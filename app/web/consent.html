Privacy & Permissions

ClaimLens Go helps you make informed food choices by analyzing menu content on delivery websites. To provide this service, we need your consent for the following:

  • Content Analysis: We scan menu items on websites you visit to detect allergens and validate claims
  • Domain Control: You choose which websites ClaimLens Go operates on through an allowlist
  • Local Storage: Your preferences and allowlisted domains are stored locally on your device
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share your browsing data

How It Works

After giving consent, you can add specific food delivery websites to your allowlist. ClaimLens Go will only scan and analyze content on those approved sites.
